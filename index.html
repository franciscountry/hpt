The Dynamic Duo

Hailing from opposite sides of the Hudson River, Hot Pants (NYC) and Tony (New Jersey) come together to create a duo that’s anything but predictable. With Hot Pants wielding a striking blue electric violin and Tony laying down soulful guitar grooves, they take the songs you love and twist them into something fresh, unexpected, and totally unforgettable. This isn’t your typical violin-and-guitar act—there’s no fiddle music here! Instead, they put their own bold, high-energy spin on classic rock, blues, folk, and more, reimagining everything from beloved anthems to deep-cut favorites with soaring harmonies and electrifying arrangements.

Behind the retro swagger, Hot Pants & Tony are the alter egos of acclaimed performers Deni Bonet and Chris Flynn, whose long-running musical partnership has taken them from Carnegie Hall and the White House, to the Great Wall of China and stages across the U.S. and Ireland. Together and individually, they have shared bills with artists including Cyndi Lauper, R.E.M., Mundy, Sarah McLachlan, The Chieftains, and members of The Pogues and Black 47.
