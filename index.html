The Dynamic Duo

Hailing from opposite sides of the Hudson River, Hot Pants (NYC) and Tony (New Jersey) come together to create a duo that’s anything but predictable. With Hot Pants wielding a striking blue electric violin and Tony laying down soulful guitar grooves, they take the songs you love and twist them into something fresh, unexpected, and totally unforgettable. This isn’t your typical violin-and-guitar act—there’s no fiddle music here! Instead, they put their own bold, high-energy spin on classic rock, blues, folk, and more, reimagining everything from beloved anthems to deep-cut favorites with soaring harmonies and electrifying arrangements. Known for their playful stage presence, powerhouse vocals, and jaw-dropping musicianship, Hot Pants & Tony have a knack for making every performance feel intimate, exciting, and full of surprises. Whether they’re delivering a soul-stirring ballad or turning a familiar hit into something you never saw coming, they guarantee a show that’s as thrilling as it is fun. So grab a pint, warm up your singing voice, and get ready to experience your favorite songs like never before. Because when a blue violin meets a rockin’ guitar, anything can happen!

Behind the retro swagger, Hot Pants & Tony are the alter egos of acclaimed performers Deni Bonet and Chris Flynn, whose long-running musical partnership has taken them from Carnegie Hall and the White House, to the Great Wall of China and stages across the U.S. and Ireland. Together and individually, they have shared bills with artists including Cyndi Lauper, R.E.M., Mundy, Sarah McLachlan, The Chieftains, and members of The Pogues and Black 47. With Hot Pants & Tony, they bring that same world-class musicianship and chemistry to a wildly fun, crowd-pleasing covers show built for packed pubs, lively festivals, and unforgettable nights.
